Anteaters

Anteaters are really good swimmers. They poke their nose out of the water like a snorkel to breathe while swimming!

Sea Otters

Sea otters have the densest fur of any animal so they stay warm in the ocean. They are one of the few animals that use tools: they crack open mollusks with a rock!

Axolotl

Axolotls are pretty chill. Unlike other amphibians, they become adults without undergoing metamorphosis. They don't really feel the need to change what ain't broken.
